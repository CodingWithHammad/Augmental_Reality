How can learners progress through levels in IMMERSE?

IMMERSE offers Levels 1 (pre A1), Level 2 (A1), Level 3 (A2), Level 4 (B1), Level 5 (B2), and Level 6 (C1). Each level correlates to the CEFR scale, the Global Scale of English, and the ACTFL Proficiency Guidelines. Within each level, you are exposed to a wide variety of language functions which allow you to complete communicative tasks. Functions include making requests, describing, apologizing, negotiating and more. It is the communicative task that drives learning. Between levels, the skills and structures are spiraled, meaning that learners will encounter the same functions in various levels, but it will build in complexity over time. Learners make progress by completing live classes, AI-powered practice, and community events in our virtual world.

What is IMMERSE's language learning methodology?

Our methodology is rooted in the principles of communicative language teaching (CLT), prioritizing authentic communication and real-world use. Each activity is designed around a specific communicative task, ensuring that learners are actively engaged in meaningful language practice. Following a backwards design approach, we begin by identifying the desired communicative outcome. This enables us to carefully select the language and structures that are essential for achieving this goal. The lesson is then structured to scaffold the learning process, starting with receptive activities to expose learners to the target language and building towards productive practice. Key elements of our methodology include: 1) Task-based learning: Lessons are centered around authentic communicative tasks, providing learners with opportunities to use language in meaningful contexts. 2) Scaffolding: The lessons are carefully structured to provide learners with the necessary support and guidance at each stage of the learning process. 3) Focus on practical communication: We emphasize the importance of effective communication over grammar rules and memorization. 4) Emergent language: Lessons are designed to allow for natural language acquisition and the development of creative language use. By combining these elements, Immerse offers a unique and effective language learning experience that prepares learners to communicate confidently in the target language.
